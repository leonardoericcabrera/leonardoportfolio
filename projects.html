TMS Evaluation

🤖 NMT Engine Pilot — Bird & Bird (Patent Translation)
MT / PEMT
Middlebury Institute of International Studies · Team project with Bowen Yuan, V Reiter & Fengshi Xu · 2026
Co-led a pilot to validate a custom-trained Neural Machine Translation engine for a leading international law firm, streamlining the translation of patent applications and IP documents from English into Spanish. The project ran 8 rounds of NMT training using a legal parallel corpus of 12,908 sentence pairs sourced from EUIPO, transitioning the firm from traditional human translation to a PEMT workflow. The pilot achieved an MQM score of ~98/100 with zero critical errors, and is projected to deliver a 30–40% reduction in cost per word and a 30–40% increase in translation throughput.
